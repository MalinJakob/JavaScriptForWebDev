Malin Jakob

This page is about me, Malin Jakob

Hello! My name is Malin Jakob, and I am a sporty and very active 26-year-old. I currently live in Gothenburg, but I’m originally from Malmö in Skåne. I lived in Malmö with my parents and older sister. My family but mostly school then dance then some more dance. I was dancing around 35 hours per week, crazy huh? Before my adventures in Gothenburg, I lived alone in Stockholm to become a professional dancer at the Royal Swedish Ballet School. My training lasted for three years, and I graduated with a professional dancer’s diploma in June 2018. On that note, I would say I am a girl with many homes!
I have worked as both a figure skating coach and a dance teacher since I was 10 years old, and I’ve been training myself since I was 3 . I have always been a highly engaged and motivated person.
Besides my passion for training and dance, I also run a business with my partner. We operate a small web agency called MW Webbdesign, and we have created websites for, among others, the Chalmers student portal for Industrial Engineering. I create the design, and my partner takes care of the system development.
