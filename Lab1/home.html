Malin Jakob

This page is about me, Malin Jakob

Hello! My name is Malin Jakob, and I am a sporty and very active 26-year-old. I currently live in Gothenburg, but I’m originally from Malmö in Skåne. I lived in Malmö with my parents and older sister. In Malmö I hung out with my family but then mostly school then dance then some more dance. I was dancing around 35 hours per week, crazy huh? Now I live with my fiance William and our child Walter. Before my adventures in Gothenburg, I lived alone in Stockholm to become a professional dancer at the Royal Swedish Ballet School. My training lasted for three years, and I graduated with a professional dancer’s diploma in June 2018. On that note, I would say I am a girl with many homes!
I have worked as both a figure skating coach and a dance teacher since I was 10 years old, and I’ve been training myself since I was 3 . I have always been a highly engaged and motivated person.
Besides my passion for training and dance, I also run a business with my partner. We operate a small web agency called MW Webbdesign, and we have created websites for, among others, the Chalmers student portal for Industrial Engineering. I create the design, and my partner takes care of the system development.

New adventure

Currently, I am studying my third year of my bachelor Cognitive Science at the University of Gothenburg. Within my program you can change some courses and right now I changed courses so that I could learn HTML and CSS. This is a big turn for me, before I have always been about the ind och the body but now i want to learn more about the technical part and the coding part. We have had some programming before in Python and some coding in our AI courses but I have never really liked it until now. I don't know if it is me, having a baby and sometimes off, but something happened during my maternity leave. So now… A new adventure begins with me starting to understand some code and then applying for my master in Interaction Design. Very exciting!
