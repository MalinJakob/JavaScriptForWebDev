My history

My journey began at four years of age when I stated to dance ballet. I feel in love with the art form and I started to work harder and I got better and better. I knew that this is what I am made for. Standing on stage and perform, maybe not ballet but some sort of dance. When I got in to the Royal Swedish Ballet School, my life was set. The direction my life was going was set in stone, or so I thought. Then all of the sudden my passion for dance died and it just did not feel good anymore. Life crisis deluxe...Years past and I found a new passion and hunger for more information about how we operate and how the mind work. In that extension I really missed being creative and thats when I found Cognitive Science and saw a chance to have it all. So a new path was set, first Cognitive Science and after that a master in Interaction Design and then, who knows. Hopefully a job where I can apply the knowledge I have and also being creative!
