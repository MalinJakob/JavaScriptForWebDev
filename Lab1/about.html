Who am I?

Hello! My name is Malin Jakob, and I am a sporty and very active 26-year-old. I currently live in Gothenburg, but I’m originally from Malmö in Skåne. Before my adventures in Gothenburg, I lived alone in Stockholm to become a professional dancer at the Royal Swedish Ballet School. My training lasted for three years, and I graduated with a professional dancer’s diploma in June 2018. On that note, I would say I am a girl with many homes!

Besides my passion for training and dance, I also run a business with my partner. We operate a small web agency called MW Webbdesign, and we have created websites for, among others, the Chalmers student portal for Industrial Engineering. I create the layout, and focus on the user experience.

My history

My journey began at four years of age when I stated to dance ballet. I feel in love with the art form and I started to work harder and I got better and better. I knew that this is what I was made for. Made for standing on stage and perform, maybe not ballet but some sort of dance. When I got in to the Royal Swedish Ballet School, my life was set. The direction my life was going was set in stone, or so I thought. Then all of the sudden my passion for dance died and it just did not feel good anymore. Life crisis deluxe...Years past and I found a new passion and hunger for more information about how we operate and how the mind work. In that extension I really missed being creative and that is when I found Cognitive Science and saw a chance to have it all. So a new path was set, first Cognitive Science and after that probably a master in Interaction Design and then, who knows. Hopefully a job where I can apply the knowledge I have and also being creative!
